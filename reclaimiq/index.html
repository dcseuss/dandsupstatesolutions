RECLAIMIQ

The engineered workflow that tracks, measures, and optimizes every pallet through intake, grading, repair, and delivery — turning reclaimed inventory into measurable value.

RECLAIMIQ WORKFLOW

THE COMPLETE CLOSED‑LOOP SYSTEM

Every pallet is tracked from intake to delivery using engineering‑driven grading, routing, and quality control.

01 —

INTAKE & TAGGING

Pallets are scanned, tagged, and logged with condition, origin, destination, and customer data — creating a traceable lifecycle record.

02 —

SORTING & GRADING

Each pallet is graded using engineering thresholds for structural integrity, repairability, and material value.

03 —

REPAIR & REBUILD

Pallets are routed to repair, rebuild, or tear‑down lines based on grade, maximizing reclaimed material and minimizing waste.

04 —

QUALITY CONTROL

Every pallet is inspected, load‑tested, and verified before re‑entering circulation.

05 —

DELIVERY

Finished pallets are staged, loaded, and delivered back into customer supply chains with full traceability.

ENGINEERING LOGIC

GRADE‑BASED DECISION ENGINE

ReclaimIQ uses engineering thresholds to determine the most efficient and profitable path for every pallet.

  • Structural integrity scoring
  • Board and stringer condition analysis
  • Repair vs. rebuild cost modeling
  • Material recovery optimization
  • Customer‑specific grade requirements
REPORTING & OPTIMIZATION

REAL‑TIME DASHBOARDS

ReclaimIQ provides live visibility into reclaimed value, waste reduction, repair efficiency, and lane performance.

  • Reclaimed value tracking
  • Repair efficiency metrics
  • Material recovery reporting
  • Customer‑specific dashboards
  • Daily, weekly, and monthly summaries
