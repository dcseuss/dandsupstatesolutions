S-03 RECLAIMIQ

RECLAIMIQ WORKFLOW

ReclaimIQ tracks pallet movement, condition, and value across your network, turning reclaimed inventory into a measurable asset.

01. INTAKE & TAGGING

Pallets are scanned, tagged, and logged into the system with condition, origin, and destination data.

02. GRADING & ROUTING

Each pallet is graded and routed to repair, reuse, or recycling based on engineering and economic thresholds.

03. REPORTING & OPTIMIZATION

Real-time dashboards show reclaimed value, waste reduction, and performance across sites and lanes.
